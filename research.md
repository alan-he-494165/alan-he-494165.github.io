## Research Interests

I am interested in organic synthesis, reaction mechanism analysis, and computational chemistry for understanding and predicting reactivity.

## Current and Recent Projects

### UROP — Spivey Group, Imperial College London (2025–current)

**Project:** Dimerisation of 1-arylmethylenecyclopropane (1-Ar-MCP)

- Contributing to development of a scalable synthetic approach for 1-Ar-MCP substrates
- Investigating mechanistic origins of unusual reactivity using computational tools
- Running parallel computational studies on novel diazirine reactivity

### UROP — Spivey Group, Imperial College London (2024)

**Project:** Dimerisation of 1-arylmethylenecyclopropane (1-Ar-MCP)

- Performed gram-scale synthesis of 1-Ar-MCP substrates (up to 10 g scale)
- Investigated a cascade rearrangement involving non-classical carbocation chemistry
- Presented project outcomes at Imperial undergraduate research fair (Oct 2024)

### Summer Research — Ganose Group, Imperial College London (2025–current)

**Project:** Benchmarking crystal structure prediction methodologies

- Comparing and evaluating computational workflows for periodic solid-state systems
- Focusing on reproducibility and method-performance tradeoffs

## Methods and Technical Skills

- **Synthesis:** Schlenk technique, pyrophoric reagent handling, glovebox workflows, multi-step synthesis
- **Characterisation:** NMR (1H, 13C, COSY, HSQC, HMBC), MS (EI/ESI/LC-MS/GC-MS), UV-Vis, fluorescence, IR, CW-EPR
- **Purification:** Flash chromatography, Biotage auto-purification, TLC, recrystallisation, distillation
- **Computation:** Gaussian, ORCA, Avogadro, VMD, Multiwfn, xtb
- **Programming/Data:** Python (including ML tooling), HPC cluster workflows, Unix command line
